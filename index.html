ABOUT INNOPROM

Innoprom is a value driven South African domiciled multi-disciplinary solutions company. Innoprom provides solutions in Project management, asset management, civil works, telecommunications, power, data analytics, and managed services . At the heart of our existence is the quest to add tremendous value to clients through innovative ideas and custom solutions. These include measures to increase operational efficiency and cost savings; revenue generation and realizing sustained optimal value of their assets. As a company we are committed to cost-efficient transactions that will satisfy all parties involved. We do this through deliberate, strategic partnerships with specialist technology providers. Innoprom brings to the fore an astute ability to re-engineer the architecture of value chain structures to achieve better efficiencies; a process which will in turn result in attainment of value for money for buyers and enhanced profitability for our partners.
